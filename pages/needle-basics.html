About Needle Gauges

The gauge number indicates the needle's diameter - the higher the number, the thinner the needle.

18-20 Gauge (Large Drawing Needles)

  • Diameter: 1.27mm - 0.91mm
  • Common lengths: 1" (25mm), 1.5" (38mm)
  • Primary use: Drawing thick medications
  • Example: Drawing testosterone cypionate
  • Source: Clinical Practice Guidelines for Drawing Medications (2017)

21-23 Gauge (Standard IM Injection)

  • Diameter: 0.82mm - 0.64mm
  • Common lengths: 1" (25mm), 1.5" (38mm)
  • Primary use: Intramuscular injections
  • Example: Testosterone IM injection
  • Source: Intramuscular Injection Techniques (2022)

25-27 Gauge (Fine Needles)

  • Diameter: 0.51mm - 0.41mm
  • Common lengths: 5/8" (16mm), 1/2" (13mm)
  • Primary use: Subcutaneous injections
  • Example: Estradiol valerate SubQ injection
  • Source: Subcutaneous Injection Guidelines (2020)
